Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI feedback system work?

Our AI feedback system uses advanced natural language processing to evaluate your practice answers based on AQA's mark schemes. It analyzes your response for historical knowledge, analysis, and argumentative structure, then provides personalized feedback to help you improve. The system also generates model answers to show you what a top-scoring response looks like.

Is this resource aligned with the latest AQA GCSE History specification?

Yes, all our content is aligned with the current AQA GCSE History specification for the "America, 1920-1973: Opportunity and inequality" period study. We regularly review and update our materials to ensure they reflect any changes to the curriculum or examination requirements.

How can I track my progress?

We're currently developing a progress tracking feature that will allow you to monitor your performance across different question types and topics. In the meantime, we recommend keeping a record of your practice question scores and reviewing the feedback to identify patterns in your strengths and areas for improvement.

Can teachers use this platform with their classes?

Yes, many teachers use our platform as a supplementary resource in their classrooms. We offer school subscriptions that provide additional features like class management, student progress tracking, and custom question setting. Please contact schools@aqahistoryrevision.com for more information about our educational institution options.

Will you be adding content for other GCSE History topics?

Yes, we plan to expand our coverage to include other AQA GCSE History topics in the future. Our next additions will likely include the "Conflict and tension" wider world depth studies and the "Britain: Health and the people" thematic study. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when new content becomes available.
